Miniature schnauser love and protect their pack. They are reliable guard dogs. The dogs are not aggresive and can coexist with other dogs just fine. Miniature schnausers can live in an appartment, bydlet v bytě, but can get bored. They will happily join you for outdoor activities or sports and have surprisingly high stamina.

They are smart and learn quickly, but be consistent and strict. Schnausers like children, but can express displeasure about treatment. Average weight is about 5 - 7 kg.
